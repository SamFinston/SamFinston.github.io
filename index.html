About Me

I like to write. Whether it's code, journalism, or poetry, you can be sure that I'm doing my best work. I'm currently pursuing a degree in Game Design and Development at the Rochester Institute of Technology, and I'm looking to gain industry experience in software development.

As a software developer, I solve problems with an ever-increasing toolkit. My coursework has me well-versed in languages from Java to JavaScript and C# to C++. Of course, I'm quick to pick up new skills, which I practice in both team and independent projects.

As a writer and game designer, I bring a new level of creativity to all the work I do. My writing has been featured in both print and online publications, and I was even chosen to perform a piece of my own on stage at the inaugural ceremony of RIT's current president.

As a student, I am a hard worker. I maintain a 4.0 GPA and Dean's List status. I am currently searching for a co-op for the summer of 2019. If you're interested in reaching out, you can contact me and check out my skills and projects below.
